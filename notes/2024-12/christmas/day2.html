fun facts:
- two turtle doves are a symbol of love, devotion, and fidelity, mating for life and living together, nurturing their children with total love.
- they represent the concept of balance and partnership, as they emphasize the importance of working together and maintaining balance in various aspects of life.
- some believe that the turtles are messengers from the spirit world or symbols of new beginnings, as they live both in water and on land.
- two turtle doves are mentioned in various scriptures, including their offering as a sacrifice for purification after childbirth in Leviticus 12:8.
- Jesus was presented in the temple 8 days after his birth with the sacrifice of two trutle doves, and he eventually came to represent purity, love and ultimate sacrifice.
